TRANCE MISSION GAME

RHYTHM, AREA-CONTROL GAME MADE FOR GLOBAL GAME JAM 2018. THE GAME IS A LOCAL 2-PLAYER GAME WHERE ONE PLAYER IS A DEER AND THE OTHER IS A MUSHROOM EACH WITH THEIR OWN MECHANICS FOR TAKING AREAS AND GAINING POINTS. YOU CAN MOVE ONLY IF YOU HIT THE BEAT OF THE MUSIC! THE GOAL IS TO GET 200 POINTS.

THE GAME WAS MADE WITH UNITY 3D IN A GROUP OF 5 WHERE I WAS ONE OF THE GAMEPLAY PROGRAMMERS.
