FILL-THE-GRID

A SIMPLE PUZZLE GAME WHERE THE GOAL IS TO FILL A GRID OF TILES. TAPPING A TILE WILL FILL OR CLEAR NEARBY TILES DEPENDING ON THE SELECTED FORMATION.

DEVELOPED WITH UNITY3D.

THE GAME CAN ALSO BE PLAYED ON KONGREGATE.
NOTE THAT GOOGLE CHROME SEEMS TO HAVE TROUBLE LOADING/DISPLAYING THE ASSETS SO I RECOMMEND PLAYING THE GAME ON ANOTHER BROWSER (I.E FIREFOX).
