UNISON

GAME JAM-SIZED GAME MADE FOR AN INTRODUCTORY COURSE UNDERSTANDING MEDIA, ART AND DESIGN A RELAXING GAME WHERE YOU GATHER SOUND FRAGMENTS AROUND THE MAP TO BUILD A MELODY.

UNISON WAS DESIGNED TO BE PART OF A 2-DAY EXHIBITION BY THE MEDIA LAB OF AALTO UNIVERSITY, WHERE THE THE VISITORS WOULD COLLECTIVELY GATHER DIFFERENT SOUNDS AND PRODUCE A COMBINED MELODY ACROSS THE WHOLE DURATION OF THE EXHIBITION.

THE GAME WAS MADE WITH UNITY 3D IN A GROUP OF 7 AND I WAS ONE OF THE GAMEPLAY PROGRAMMERS.
